19 june 2022

Is din ke bare me mai jyada sochta nhi hu kyuki pta nhi kyu thodi sad wali feeling ati hai. Abhi mai type kar rha to bhi mujhe halka halka ajeeb lag rha.

iske just 3-4 din pehle mai bhut badi galti kardiya tha , tu bahut naraz ho gayi thi, fir jaise taise manaya. iske 3-4 din baad bhi firse kuch hua jiske bare me kabhi galti se bhi khayal a jata to bahut bura lagta hai, aur shraddha bhi bahut roi thi us din.... Aur sbse jyada kharab isliye kyuki is din ko yaad karke mai un 5 mahino me bahut baar roya hu... iske pure 5 mahine baad mai tujhe dekh paya tha...

Khair to humne decide kiya tha ki hum dono Bhaskar sir k ghar sath jayenge aur isi tarah mil lenge. vaise mera man sir ke ghar jane ka bilkul tha nhi mujhe to bas tujhse milna tha. Fir mai tujhe manaya ki humlog pehle thodi der kahi chalenge uske baad bhaskar sir ke yaha jayenge. Us din mausam bhi subah se hi kharab type ka tha. Mai bas ye prayer kar rha tha ki bhagwan barish na ho....

firse kuch bahana maar ke ghar se nikla, fir tujhe gadi me baithaya shop k samne se aur fir humlog wapas usi jagah jane ki soche(30 may wali) lekin fir soche ki thoda aur aage chalte hai. Fir gaadi tu chala rahi thi... Humlog road k end tak gaye aur wapas mud kar aa rhe the utne me samne se ek gaadi ayi aur tune scooty road se neeche utar di, ab road k neeche mitti thi, to balance bigda aur hum dono gir gaye.

sachme us samay bahut acha laga tha jab tu khadi hogayi aur tujhe kuch nhi hua tha, mai ekdum dar hi gaya tha. Khair kuch hua to khaas nahi bas gadi gandi ho gayi. Fir humlogo ne gadi ko usi spot pe dhula fir wapas usi road me jane lage. Fir halki halki barish start ho rahi thi to humlog ek shed ke neeche baith gaye. shraddha ne fir mujhe ache se hath se pakda aur kandhe par sir bhi rakha.... mai to pura numb hogaya tha kuch soch hi nhi pa rha tha

fir maine bhi shraddha ko pakda thode ajeeb tareeke se, acha bahut laga tha lekin is moment ko hi yaad karke bhut baar roya tha agle kuch mahino tak... khair abtak to bhaskar sir wala plan cancel ho chuka tha, fir mai tujhe chora aur apne apne ghar chale gaye aur us din hum dono hi ache nhi dikh rahe the....
